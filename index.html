About me

Hey! I'm Anangsha. Currently I'm doing a PhD in neurocognitive psychology at Sunshine Coast, Australia.

I am also a psychological consultant and mentor at Likeminded, with several years of clinical experience. I've worked at psychiatric hospitals, clinics, as well as community and mental health settings all over India, Spain and Germany.

My specializations include Clinical Neuropsychology, Developmental Psychopathology, Counseling, Cognitive Behavior Therapy, Solution-Focused Behavior Therapy and Stress Management along with yoga and mindfulness-based practices.
